Our more than 20 years of experience

Lion Kithul Wood is a small family business in southern Sri Lanka that produces creative and customized housewares from kithul wood and coconut wood. They are committed to using sustainably sourced local materials and providing natural, durable alternatives to imported plastic items. Products include wooden spoons, forks, chopsticks, plates, bowls, cups, trays, table mats and runners, soap dishes, storage containers, and furniture. Lion Kithul Wood assists poor children from single-parent families with tuition fees and school supplies.
